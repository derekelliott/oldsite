THOUGHTFUL DESIGN

Derek Elliott is an award winning furniture designer with roots in Product Design that branch into the fields of environment design and digital design. Derek has worked on a number of projects both professionally with his first employer, Bernhardt Design, as well as personally as a freelance designer. Derek utilizes his 12 years of experience in 3D to create stunning photorealistic visualizations for his own furniture projects as well as images for product presentations and even scientific diagrams. Please get in touch by emailing derek@derrk.com
